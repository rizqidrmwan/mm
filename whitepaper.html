The Mythology

HISTORICAL ORIGINS

The Creature

In Greek mythology, the Chimera was a monstrous fire-breathing hybrid creature, typically depicted with the head and body of a lion, a goat's head emerging from its back, and a serpent for a tail.

The Legend

Said to be the offspring of Typhon and Echidna, the Chimera ravaged the lands of Lycia until it was slain by the hero Bellerophon riding the winged horse Pegasus.
